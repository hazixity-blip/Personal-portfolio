Lab Work

Experiments & findings.

Microbiology

Antimicrobial Efficacy Study

Tested the antimicrobial properties of alcohol, chlorine, and a combined solution on wild soil samples to evaluate comparative effectiveness.

Key finding: The combined solution acted as an antagonist — reducing overall antimicrobial efficiency rather than enhancing it.
Mycology

Fruit Peel Substrate Study

Tested dragon fruit, pineapple, and pomegranate peel substrates on Aspergillus niger and a related species to identify optimal growth substrates.

Key finding: Mixed and pomegranate substrates performed best. Dragon fruit peel showed the least effectiveness.
